Academic Policy

1. Academic Integrity

Aluno is committed to upholding the highest standards of academic integrity. Students are expected to complete all quizzes and exams honestly and without unauthorized assistance.

2. Cheating and Plagiarism

Any form of cheating or plagiarism is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, copying answers from others, using unauthorized resources, or submitting work that is not your own.

3. Consequences of Violations

Violations of the academic policy may result in penalties, including but not limited to failing the quiz or exam, suspension from the platform, or other disciplinary actions as deemed appropriate by the administration.

4. Reporting Violations

Students and instructors are encouraged to report any suspected violations of the academic policy to the administration for review.

5. Review and Updates

This academic policy is subject to periodic review and updates. Users will be notified of any significant changes.
